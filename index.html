Who We Are

We have all your needs, from micro macro

BuildMO is a well reputed, and rapidly growing construction company in Sri Lanka. With unique and innovative architectures, we as constructors have been able to stand out from the norm. Client interaction and satisfaction, throughout the building process is a motto followed by BuildMO at all times. With our ever-growing customer base and love for construction, BuildMO will be your one stop for all your building dreams in Si Lanka.

Residential Constructions

Whatever your dream house maybe, we at BuildMO are here to build it. Using our expert knowledge in residential constructing, we are always able to deliver the most astonishing homes to our clients using the best designs and materials in the most cost-effective manner. Check out some of our projects to see what wonders our team is possible of achieving.

Commercial Buildings

Given the clear thoughts and motives, we at BuildMO is capable of building the most modern commercial buildings to fit the new day and age. We are always up-to-date with construction trends from around the world, which helps our commercial buildings to be quite unique among the rest that’s found in Sri Lanka.

All Kind of Architectural Buildings

BuildMO is passionate about architecture and love a good challenge. Over the years of experience in studying, building and designing we have come to know the ins and outs of various mind-blowing architectural buildings, making BuildMo specialize in building monumental architectural buildings to your heart’s desire.

Tempered glass facades

Most buildings are judged by its facades, and we at BuildMO proudly boast of our specialization in tempered glass facades to be among the best in Sri Lanka. Making the interior of your building protected from unhealthy sun rays while helping everyone inside to be a part of the nature tempered glassed facades has become one of the most popular requirements of our clients lately.

All kind of Ceilings

From conventional ceilings to suspended ceilings, we at BuildMO specialize in a variety of ceiling types. Using the best materials most fit for any type of construction, we at BuildMO are known for our perfection in building world class ceilings with a profound durability. Making beauty and function complement each other, we are pleased to serve you at any type of ceiling needs.

Aluminum Doors and windows

Need to stand out of the more traditional buildings with wooden doors and windows? BuildMO provides the best alternative, providing you astonishing doors and windows made with the finest aluminum found in Sri Lanka. Our range of aluminum doors and windows could give your building a fresh look, while ensuring the durability for times to come.

Timber Flooring

Timber floors has hit the world of construction like a storm, with its high demands in the modern architectural world. As a construction company who keeps up with modern trends, and especially who loves to put a huge smile on our client’s face, we at BuildMO has also perfectioned the art of timber floors, guaranteeing high durability, while guiding you with proper maintenance materials.

Steel Fabricating

Stepping out of the more conventional aspects of constructing, we at BuildMO has an expert team on all your steel fabricating needs as well. From steel equipment’s to steel frames, BuildMO will build all your steel needs perfecting each aspect as per the client requirement. While always constructing to fulfill client desires, BuildMo has become the one stop even for steel fabricating.
